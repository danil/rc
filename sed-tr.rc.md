## Inplace replacement

    sed --in-place 's/your_old_string/your_new_string/g' path/to/file

## Remove the 7rd line

    sed --in-place '7d' ~/.ssh/known_hosts

## Remove 3-7 lines

    sed '3,7d' path/to/in/file > path/to/out/file
